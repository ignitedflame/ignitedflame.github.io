About Me

Alyssa Cinda’s life philosophy is centered on the principle of ‘aligning’, and with this she empowers female entrepreneurs to build profitable lifestyles by “energetically aligning to their dreams”.

Having successfully aligned her own energy to her dreams, Alyssa makes energetic alignment simple and achievable. She works exclusively with  female entrepreneurs who are ready to “awaken. release. align.”.

Through the power of energetic alignment, Alyssa equips women to embrace their dreams and release toxic energy and mindsets with her powerful audio clearings.

With more aware females than ever before, Alyssa believes the alignment adventure is “truly a soul’s journey” and has worked to foster a community of  female entrepreneurs who are active aligners.

This is my Mission

To empower and equip female entrepreneurs globally to create their own soul aligned lifestyle and to enable them to have the impact on the world they were created to have.
